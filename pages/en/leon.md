## León as a cybersecurity hub

### INCIBE

The province of León is home to the headquarters of **INCIBE (Instituto Nacional de Ciberseguridad)**, Spain's national reference body for cybersecurity. INCIBE is responsible for:

- **INCIBE-CERT** — the national incident response team for citizens and businesses
- **Training and awareness programs** in cybersecurity
- **Cyberex** and other national cybersecurity exercises
- **Supporting the entrepreneurial ecosystem** in cybersecurity

INCIBE's presence has generated an **ecosystem of cybersecurity companies** around it, turning León into an attraction pole for security professionals.

### The ecosystem

Around INCIBE, a business fabric has developed that includes:

- Security consulting and auditing firms
- Cybersecurity startups
- Specialized training centers
- Professionals who have chosen León as their base for remote work in the security sector

León proves that you don't need to be in Madrid or Barcelona to be at the forefront of Spanish cybersecurity.

## Routes, gastronomy and hidden gems

León is much more than cybersecurity. It's a province with:

- **Mountain routes** — Picos de Europa, Ancares, Babia
- **The Camino de Santiago** — several stages cross the province
- **El Barrio Húmedo** — possibly the best tapas district in Spain
- **Gastronomy** — cecina, botillo, morcilla de León, mantecadas de Astorga
- **Heritage** — the Cathedral, San Isidoro, Las Médulas, Astorga, Ponferrada
