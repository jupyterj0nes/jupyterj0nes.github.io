## León, cybersecurity hub

The province of León is home to the headquarters of **INCIBE (Instituto Nacional de Ciberseguridad)**, Spain's national reference body for cybersecurity. Its presence has generated an ecosystem of companies, startups and security professionals that have turned León into an attraction pole for the industry.

### INCIBE

- **INCIBE-CERT** — national incident response team for citizens and businesses
- **Training and awareness programs** in cybersecurity
- **Cyberex** and other national exercises
- **Supporting the entrepreneurial ecosystem** in cybersecurity

### The ecosystem

Around INCIBE, a business fabric has developed that includes security consulting firms, startups, training centers and professionals who work remotely from León.

León proves that you don't need to be in Madrid or Barcelona to be at the forefront of Spanish cybersecurity.

## The province

León is much more than cybersecurity. It's a province with a millenary history, unique heritage and breathtaking nature:

- **The Cathedral** — one of the jewels of European Gothic
- **San Isidoro** — the Sistine Chapel of Romanesque art
- **Las Médulas** — UNESCO World Heritage, ancient Roman gold mine
- **Picos de Europa** — high mountain at its purest
- **The Camino de Santiago** — several stages cross the province
- **Astorga and Ponferrada** — Templar and Maragato history
