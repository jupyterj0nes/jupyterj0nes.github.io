## Routes

The province of León is a paradise for mountain and nature lovers:

- **Picos de Europa** — Cares gorge, Fuente Dé, Lakes of Covadonga
- **Ancares leoneses** — pristine Atlantic forests
- **Babia** — Biosphere Reserve, the Spanish expression "estar en Babia" (daydreaming) makes sense once you visit
- **La Cabrera** — León's best kept secret
- **The Camino de Santiago** — the Leonese stages are among the most beautiful

## Gastronomy

León is arguably the best province in Spain for food. Some highlights:

- **El Barrio Húmedo** — Spain's best tapas district. Order a beer, get a free tapa. That simple.
- **Cecina de León** — mountain-air cured beef with protected designation of origin
- **Botillo del Bierzo** — the hearty winter dish of El Bierzo
- **Morcilla de León** — blood sausage with rice or onion, always spectacular
- **Mantecadas de Astorga** — the quintessential Maragato sweet
- **Sopas de ajo** — garlic soup, the perfect recovery after a mountain hike
