## Facendera Hacking

*Facendera* is a word from the Leonese language meaning **community work** — when the whole village came together to do a task that benefited everyone. That's the spirit of this community: sharing knowledge, helping each other, and growing cybersecurity in León.

Facendera Hacking is the underground cybersecurity community of the province of León. A meeting point for professionals, students and enthusiasts of hacking and information security.

---

## What do we do?

- **Meetups** and informal gatherings
- **Technical talks** on DFIR, pentesting, threat hunting, malware analysis
- **CTFs** and security challenges
- **Networking** among industry professionals
